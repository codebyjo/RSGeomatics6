RS Geomatics is Professional Land Surveying firm carried out Cadastral, Engineering and Geodetic, construction layout and GIS services in GTA and surrounding area. We are member with Association of Ontario Land Surveyors. We take all necessary steps to protect public interest.

We carry out Cadastral Surveying, Engineering and Geodetic Surveying, construction layout and GIS services.

Our staff are licenced and certified professionals and committed to continuous professional development. Our state of the art technology process the result in precise manner.

RS Geomatics treat surveying as practical profession and Surveyors as universal professionals.
